How It Works

Plans to Numbers in
4 Precise Steps

Our tested process eliminates delays. Send us your plans — we return bid-ready estimates on schedule, every time.

Submit Your Plans

Upload in any format — PDF, DWG, Revit, or scanned paper sets. Our portal handles projects of all types and scales.

Delivery & Support

Detailed Excel and PDF deliverable with full backup. We stay available throughout your entire bid and award process.
